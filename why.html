  • Emerald

  • Benefits of Emerald

    The Emerald (Panna) stone commonly known as Pachha/Panna Represents planet Mercury and its element is Earth. It is beneficial to those who belong to the zodiac sun and moon signs Gemini and Virgo. Emerald is known to give the wearer intelligence and wisdom, giving a best to one’s career. It is noted as the sign of kindness and love in medical astrology gives good health and wealth. It adds patience and consistency in the wearer’s life. It is advised that the emerald should be worn on the little finger.

  • Pearl

  • Benefits of Pearl

    Pearl (Moti) commonly known as Muthyam/Moti is the gemstone of Moon or Chardra, and its element is Water. It should be worn on the little finger and is beneficial to those belonging to the zodiac sun and moon signs Cancer. It gives the wearer a very balanced emotional Life and also keeps a check on the Temperament of the Person. Peace and Politeness is a gift of pearl and it also helps to maintain a normal body Temperature. It is known to strengthen the social status of the wearer.

  • Blue sapphire

  • Benifits of Blue sapphire

    The blue sapphire (Neelam) commonly known as Neelam/shani represents the planet Saturn/shani and its element is air. It is preferably worn by people belonging to the zodiac sun and moon signs Capricorn. It gives the wearer a long life along with knowledge and discipline. It saves from enviousness of others and also provides with mental peace. It is very popular gemstone among the masses as it is known to remove financial hurdles from the wearer’s life. It should be worn on the middle finger. this stone should be test before wear.

  • Hessonite

  • Benifits of Hessonite

    The Hessonite (Gomed )is the gemstone of Rahu with Ether as its element. It is beneficial to those who fall under the zodiac sun and moon signs Aquarius. Once worn the stone gives positive results of Rahu as per its position in the birth chart and thus diminishes the negative results of Rahu. It is known to release the wearer off tension and helps to recover a broken relationship. It blesses with good health, wealth and happiness and cures mental disorders. It should be worn on the middle finger..

  • Cat’s eye

  • Benifits of cat’s eye

    The gemstone cat’s eye (Lahsuniya) also known as Lehsunia resembles the eye of a cat and thus the name. It is the gemstone of Ketu and its element is water. It is beneficial to people who fall under the zodiac sun and moon signs of Pisces. It is known to eliminate the bad effects of Ketu in the birth chart and strengthen financial status of the wearer. It brings forth lavishness and happiness and is good for people hailing from the world of business as it removes the high hurdles in business and carrier.

  • Yellow sapphire

  • Benifits of yellow sapphire

    The yellow sapphire (Pukhraj) is the gemstone of Jupiter /guru and helps the wearer to harness the positive energy of the planet. Its element is Ether and is compatible with yellow gold or silver also. It is considered good for the people belonging to the zodiac sun and moon signs Sagittarius and Pisces. Its foremost quality is its ability to bring happiness. Its positive effects can be seen in the areas of monetary benefits, marital status and physical health. It should be worn on the index finger of the right hand.

  • Red Coral

  • Red Coral

    The Red Coral (Monga) also known as Moonga/Mangal, is the Gemstone of Mars and planet earth is its element. It is beneficial to people belonging to the zodiac sun and moon signs Aries and Scorpio and is compatible with metals yellow gold silver and copper. This stone is known to bring forth courageous attitude and material success to the wearer. It can infuse active traits in an inactive person. The moonga is also helpful when it comes to boosting leadership qualities and overcoming mental depression

  • Ruby

  • Ruby

    Ruby (manek) also known as Manik /Suray represents the sun, ruler of all planets and its element is fire. It has positive effects if worn by people of the zodiac sun and moon signs Leo. The wearer gains popularity and good status in his profession. It brings forth fame and name with good health. Related to creativity, passion and determination, it boosts the wearer with optimism and helps him to achieve good fortune. The Ruby is also considered as the gemstone of Love and should be worn on the ring finger

    • Diamond

    • Benefits Of Diamond

      Diamond strengthens Venus, which rules the love and married life of a person. Wearing Diamond brings passion, health, understanding and satisfaction from one’s love affair or marriage. One of the benefits of wearing Diamond is the bringing of Concord and peace into a disturbed married life. People with a strong Venus are more likely to choose an artistic or a glamorous profession like Fashion, Acting, Painting, Writing, Music, Designing etc. Wearing Diamond brings creativity and success to its wearer, especially if he/she is in such a profession. Diamond builds the confidence of its wearer. People who wear a Diamond feel more confident, charming and sure of themselves. It also helps in improving the social life of its wearer.
