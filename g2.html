Aquamarine

Description

Aquamarine is a good looking stone and substitute of diamond and neelam. It is a cool stone and keeps the mind cool and clears off all ulcers. Where there are tensions in between the lovers, wife and husband this stone be confidently used by both or can be gifted to each other for increasing love and understanding. In European countries this is to believed to ward off bad luck.

Details

  • Name : Hindi : Beruj, Sanskrit : Harit Neelamani.
  • Gem-Group : Beryl.
  • Crystal Structure : Hexagonal.
  • Composition : Beryllium Aluminum Silicate , Be3Al2(Sio3)6
  • Hardness : 7.5
  • Colour : Whitish Blue, Greenish White.
  • Planet : Venus ( sukra) and Saturn ( sani).
  • Availability : Brazil , Russia, India, Pakistan, Afghanistan.
  • Specific Gravity : 2.69
  • Refractive Index : 1.57-1.58
  • Double Refraction : 0.006
  • Lustre : Vitreous.
  • Metal : Silver.
  • Process : Purify the ring/locket ind panchamruta, cow’s fresh milkor coconut water. Lit Dhoop-deep, rite haldi ( turmeric) powder and sindoor and wear in Middle finger for sani on Saturday and ring/little finger on friday for Venus ( sukra).
  • Size : 3-4 carats , for speed action 4-6 carats.
  • Lord : Lord Bhairav ( for Bhairav ) and Goddess Lakshmi ( for Venus) be worshipped.
  • Adviced For : Lovers, people who cannot wear diamond or fear wearing Neelam.
  • Mantra : “OM DRANG DRING DROUNG SAH SUKRAYE NAMAHA”.
  • “OM HRING SHREE SHANISCHARAYE NAMAHA”.
  • Note : For good results use with Red-coral / Pearl and worship Guru Yantra.
